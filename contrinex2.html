World leader in Sensors

Contrinex is the world market leader of metal sensors resistant to high pressure and temperature with particularly demanding operations, as well as miniature sensors, long-distance sensors and detection.

Among its 6000 products are the photoelectric sensors of distance, which are measuring devices for several areas of application. For short distances, professionals use the highly accurate laser triangulation method, such as the new sensor DTL-C23, suitable for the detection of small pieces as well as in the checking of position, height or thickness. For larger distances, these opt for flight time optical technology, such as the recent-sensor DTL-C55, particularly suitable for use in mobile logistics.

These measurement technologies are used by high precision sensors, achieving a distance measurement independently of the colour or characteristics of the surface of the object to be detected and still a high repeatability. In addition, sensors are highly resistant to industrial cleaning.
