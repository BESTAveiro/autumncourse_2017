Contrinex

Contrinex will be partner of the Autumn Course “Internet of Things: Anything, Anywhere, Anytime!” organized by the association BEST Aveiro.

Founded in 1972 in Switzerland, Contrinex is a manufacturer of industrial automation sensors and it has been growing at the European, Asian, North and South American markets. The company is represented in more than 60 countries around the world.

It has approximately 6000 unique and innovative products whose characteristics far outweigh those of conventional sensors, becoming one of the leaders on the technology of photoelectric and inductive sensors, so as RFID systems and security.

Besides that, it is also the world market leader in high pressure and temperature resistant sensors with special requirements, as well as miniature sensors and long distance and detection sensors.

For further more information about Contrinex, visit: https://www.contrinex.com
